What We Do

We are Autism Partnership Foundation

Our mission is to make a difference in the life of individuals with autism.

We raise funds to generate public awareness and knowledge about autism; and advocate ABA treatment and intervention through community programs of education and research and provide support and help for children with ASD and their families in Asia.

ABA Treatment

Since the establishment of APF, we have been advocating Applied Behaviour Analysis (ABA) treatment and supporting ASD individuals, their families and all those involved to thrive for realizing inherent potential of ASD people and achieving the greatest degree of independence and highest quality of life possible.

We support ASD individuals and their family with the following services:

Children & Youth

> AP School

> 1 Month intensive Treatment

> Learning Buddies

> Aspire Programme
